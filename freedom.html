Are you tired of waisting your life away sitting in traffic? We were and knew that there had to be a more time efficient solution to transportation. Through our brainstorming we came up with the idea of personal quadracopter planes.

Our personal quadracopters can be flown over the traffic and land in parking spaces outside of your destination due to its relatively small size.

In house training.

We offer in house training so you can become a licensed drone pilot. It is affordable and a relatively easy license to obtain compared to a pilots license due to the automation of the drone devices and installed safety programming. (Parachutes not included)
